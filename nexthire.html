Core Features

Everything Recruitment Needs, Reimagined

From job posting to shortlisting — every touchpoint is enhanced by AI, structured by data, and built for speed.

🧠
AI Resume Analysis
Parses PDF and DOCX files to extract skills, experience, and qualifications. Scores each resume against the specific job's requirements — not a generic rubric.
💬
Behavioural Intelligence
Generates up to 15 role-tailored questions per job. Analyses free-text responses using NLP to infer personality, sentiment, and soft-skill indicators.
📊
Multi-Dimensional Scoring
Combines resume score (0–100), behavioural score, and a separate fit score — all transparent, weighted, and explainable to both recruiters and candidates.
🎭
Big Five Personality Model
Maps candidate responses across Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism — giving deep culture-fit signals.
🏆
Ranked Candidate Lists
Recruiters see a ranked leaderboard of applicants per job. Filter by score, status, or fit classification. One-click to shortlist or advance to review.
📄
PDF Analysis Reports
Automatically generates a detailed PDF report per applicant containing full scoring breakdowns — securely stored and recruiter-accessible only.

AI Pipeline

How the Intelligence Works

Every candidate goes through a structured, multi-stage AI evaluation. Transparent, reproducible, and audit-ready.

01
Resume Upload & Parsing
Candidate uploads PDF or DOCX. The parser extracts structured data: skills, experience timeline, qualifications, and certifications.
FILE_PARSE → SKILL_EXTRACT
02
Resume Scoring Engine
Three sub-scores computed: skill_match (0–100), experience alignment, and qualification match. Weighted average produces the resume_score.
WEIGHTED_SCORE → resume_score
03
AI Question Generation
LLM generates 1–15 role-specific behavioural questions based on the job description and required competencies. Cached after first generation.
LLM → question_set
04
Behavioural NLP Analysis
Candidate free-text responses processed through quality validation → LLM sentiment + Big Five + soft skills inference → composite behavioural_score.
NLP → BIG_FIVE → behavioural_score
05
Final Scoring & Ranking
Combined score (resume + behavioural, 50/50 default) and independent fit_score computed. Candidate ranked within their job pool for recruiter review.
COMBINE → FIT → RANK

Scoring System

Transparent, Layered, Explainable

Four distinct scoring dimensions. Each measures something different. All are designed to be understood — not black-boxed.

Resume Score
Weighted average of skill match, experience alignment, and qualification score. Calibrated per job — not generic benchmarks.
Scale: 0 – 100 · Display as N / 100
Behavioural Score
Composite from sentiment, Big Five conscientiousness & agreeableness, and soft skill indicators. Derived entirely from natural language responses.
Scale: 0 – 1 · Display as × 100%
Combined Score
Primary ranking signal. Formula: (resume_score × 0.5) + (behavioural_score × 100 × 0.5). Configurable weights per deployment.
Scale: 0 – 100 · Primary ranking
Fit Score
Independent compatibility signal. Incorporates personality-role alignment, response-to-JD keyword coherence, and sentiment consistency.
Scale: 0 – 1 · Display as label
